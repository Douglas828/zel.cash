What is Zelcash?

Zelcash is a cryptocurrency that holds a central role in the Zel Ecosystem. It give the incentive for ZelNode ownership to enable the creation of ZelDex and the ZelDev Platform, enabling us to create a truly decentralized development platform.

Zelcash is minable making distribution fair. ZelNodes are soon to be added, and will allow holders of Zelcash to operate a ZelNode, receiving a portion of blockreward for operating it. Zelcash is the enablier in the Zel Ecosystem. It will always be a key part of the Zel Ecosystem.
